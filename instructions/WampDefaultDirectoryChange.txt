Open httpd.conf with notepad++. 
	Location: C->wamp->bin->bin->apache->apache2.4.9->conf->httpd.conf

Search for the term “DocumentRoot”
Change the DocumentRoot path to your custom directory
	Change this line: DocumentRoot "c:/wamp/www"
	Into: DocumentRoot "c:/wamp/www/Webpage"

Save your changes and “Restart All Services”
